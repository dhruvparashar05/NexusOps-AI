# BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA (BGMI): CAMPUS CLASH OFFICIAL RULEBOOK
**Document Reference: BGMI_DISPUTE_GUIDELINES_2026.docx**

Official tournament regulations for NEXUSOPS-integrated collegiate and community mobile gaming championships.

---

## 1. Squad Registration & Lobby Details
* **Teams & Roster**: Matches are played in **Squad Mode** (4 players per team). Up to 1 substitute is permitted.
* **Match Timing**: Due to academic schedules, BGMI custom rooms are hosted strictly at **06:00 PM IST**.
* **Room Launch**: Custom room keys and slot lists are automatically broadcasted to the Telegram sync group exactly 15 minutes before launch. Lobbies lock at 05:55 PM IST.

## 2. Competitive Map Pool & Scoring
* **Map Pool Rotation**: 
  * Match 1: **Erangel** (Tactical, open-field setups).
  * Match 2: **Miramar** (Long-range desert engagements).
  * Match 3: **Sanhok** (Fast-paced, close-quarter jungle combat).
* **Scoring Matrix**: Points are allocated based on placements and finishes:
  * 1st Place (Winner Winner Chicken Dinner): **15 Points**
  * 2nd Place: **12 Points**
  * 3rd Place: **10 Points**
  * Finish / Kill points: **1 Point per finish** (multiplier applies in early zones).

## 3. Mandatory Safety & Anti-Emulator Policy
* **Mobile Clients Only**: Players must use official, verified BGMI mobile clients on Android or iOS mobile devices.
* **Emulator Ban**: Use of PCs, tablets, iPads, emulators (like BlueStacks, GameLoop), or third-party controller adaptors is **strictly prohibited**.
* **Anti-Cheat Scans**: The NEXUSOPS Telegram integration will auto-audit player clients. Any player detected on an emulator client will face immediate squad disqualification.
