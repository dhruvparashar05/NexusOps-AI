# VALORANT CHAMPIONS TOUR: COMMUNITY ARENA RULEBOOK
**Document Reference: VAL_COMMUNITY_RULEBOOK_V4.pdf**

Welcome to the official NEXUSOPS-managed Valorant tournament guidelines. This rulebook is referenced dynamically by automated referee bots and dispute triaging agents.

---

## 1. Roster and Substitutions
* **Squad Size**: Teams must consist of 5 primary players and up to a maximum of 2 registered substitutes.
* **Substitution Lock**: Roster changes or substitute replacements must be reported to the lobby referee at least **2 hours prior** to the lobby launch time. 
* **Approval**: The assigning referee or administrator must manually approve all roster updates. Unauthorized roster swaps will trigger automatic team disqualification.

## 2. Match Rules and Overtime Regulations
* **Format**: All standard bracket matches are played in a 5v5 Single Elimination format.
* **Match Start**: Lobbies open exactly 15 minutes before the match start time. Teams not fully joined in the custom lobby within 10 minutes of match launch will face default forfeit.
* **Tiebreakers and Overtime**: 
  * If a match reaches a **12-12 tie**, teams must play custom game overtime.
  * **MR3 Overtime Rule**: Overtime rounds are played in sets of 3 (MR3 format). Teams alternate attack/defend rounds. The first team to win 2 out of 3 overtime rounds in a set wins the overtime set.
  * **Sudden Death**: If matches remain tied after 2 overtime sets, sudden-death default parameters apply.

## 3. Disallowed Actions and Violations
* **Toxicity**: Any form of toxic language or match-chat abuse flagged by automatic toxicity sweeps will result in an immediate warning. A second infraction leads to lobby muting or full disqualification.
* **Cheating / Exploits**: Use of unauthorized game client exploits, third-party overlay aim assistants, or bugs banned by Riot Games is strictly prohibited. Suspicious plays will be auto-triaged by the AI Dispute assistant.
